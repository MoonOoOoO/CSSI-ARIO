      This category is defined as an image primarily containing close-up or overview of structural drawings. For example, images including floor plans, building schematics, and/or column dimensions are categorized in this category. In many cases, digital versions of the structural drawings for the subject buildings do not exist. Photographs of the entire set of structural drawings provide valuable reference information that will enable a better understanding of the subject building.
